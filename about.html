About Our Centre

Learn our history, mission, values, and student timeline in Dabra.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, premium quality, and job-oriented computer education to students in Dabra and surrounding rural areas. We strive to empower students with verified technical knowledge that enables quick placements in government and private sectors.

Our Vision

To be the benchmark computer training institute in the Gwalior region, recognized for educational integrity, practical training brilliance, and modern learning infrastructure under single-faculty mentorship.

Our Growth Journey

Key milestones and expansion highlights of ABC Computer Training Centre.

2016

Institute Founded

Director Vikash Sahu MCT established ABC Computer Centre in Dabra with a small laboratory and a solid vision of job-ready training.

2019

Affiliation Expansion

Obtained formal university affiliations to award recognized DCA & PGDCA diplomas, and introduced dedicated computer typing classrooms.

2022

Infrastructure Upgrade

Upgraded the laboratory to high-performance terminal bays, installed advanced cooling lines, and integrated standard ERP accounting workflows.

2026

Coding Classes Launched

Pioneered detailed software engineering classes in Dabra, covering programming foundations in C, C++, Java, and Python.
